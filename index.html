About Me

Okay, so… I’m a Computer Science student, right? And basically, I spend an unhealthy amount of time writing code and trying to make the internet a cooler place.

I kinda fell in love with building things that work, but also look nice doing it.

Like, if software development was a sport, I’d be the guy who shows up with matching shoes and still runs into a wall... but learns from it and keeps going.

Over time — through school, side projects, and way too many late nights with StackOverflow. I’ve gotten pretty comfortable working with Python, C, C++...

I also designed this portfolio myself using good ol’ HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, because why not torture myself a little?

What really gets me hyped is connecting all the pieces — from sketching a UI to writing the backend logic, then seeing it all come together like, “Wait… I made that?”

But hey, when I’m not smashing my keyboard trying to debug something, I’m probably jamming out on guitar, piano, or drums.

Or painting. Or making digital art. I’m kind of a creative mess, and I love it.

I also have experience with computer networking. Turning chaos into order, or the otherway around, JUST WITH WIFI!
